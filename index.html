Advanced Design

Unitech is a custom airhandler manufacture. As such we can start with a blank slate and let the customer hand craft any features they need to meet their needs. We have the tools and experience for any heat recovery, quiet, clean, specific size constraints, pre-packaged, stacked, tested product.

Elegant Solutions

Unitech has its own in house powder coat facility for a clean look and finish. Unitech also has an experienced and equipped in house welding and sheet meal shop. This allows Unitech to develop and customize unique solutions that are both stylish and durable.

Fully Responsive

Unitech has in house technical experience to work with consulting engineers to provide drawings, selections and specifications. Unitech will also provide quotes based on contract documents, we will do our own takeoffs and we will read and understand the design intent prior to bidding. We can also work with you on design build or selling directly to the owner.

Completely Custom
